Special Ryan Spaghetti Burrito Recipe

Description

Spaghetti in Tortilla haha

Ingredients

  • Tortilla(s)
  • Spaghetti
  • (Optional) Vegetables/Garnishes (E.g. Spinach)
  • (Optional) Sauces (E.g. Guacamole)

Steps

  1. Boil Noodle
  2. Do not burn like this!!!
  3. Put sauce and veggies in pan
  4. Sizzle sizzle
  5. Put noodle in pan and stir with sauce
  6. Put spaghetti on plate
  7. Heat up tortilla(s) on same pan for maximum efficiency
  8. Put spaghetti on tortilla and wrap good
